Why Coastline

Four things make us
actually different.

/ 01

Same technician every single visit.

Most pool companies rotate technicians by week. We don't. The person who does your first visit does every visit until they leave the company. They learn your pool's quirks, your equipment, your usage patterns — and the chemistry stabilizes because of it.

/ 02

Digital report card after every visit.

SMS to your phone within an hour of departure: chlorine reading, pH, alkalinity, calcium hardness, equipment status, anything we noticed. You see what we did. We see what you saw. No "did they actually come" mystery.

/ 03

Repairs handled in-house.

Pump went out at 9pm Friday? We have parts in the truck for the most common Pentair, Hayward, and Jandy failures. Most non-emergency repairs done on the next weekly visit. Real emergencies get a same-day or next-day truck.

/ 04

No contracts. Ever.

Monthly billing, cancel anytime. Skip November-February if you want (we handle off-season at a reduced rate). We don't believe in 12-month auto-debit contracts that punish customers who try to leave. We earn the next month every month.
