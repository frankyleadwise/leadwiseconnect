SERVICE 01 · WEEKLY MAINTENANCE
Service / 01

Weekly maintenance.

The core service. Forty-five to sixty minutes on-site every week, same technician, same day. A Tampa Bay pool needs more attention than most — humidity, summer rainfall, pollen from oaks and palms, year-round swim use. Skipping a week here is not the same as skipping a week in Ohio.

Every visit: vacuum, brush walls and tile line, skim surface, empty skimmer and pump baskets, balance chemistry (chlorine, pH, alkalinity, calcium hardness, cyanuric acid), inspect equipment, top off chemicals as needed. Digital report card SMS'd to your phone before we leave the driveway.

  • Vacuum, brush, skim weekly

  • Equipment inspection
  • Digital report card SMS
  • Chemicals included

Service / 02

Equipment repair.

Pump motors, filters, heaters, salt cells, automation panels, plumbing leaks. Most Tampa pools run Pentair, Hayward, or Jandy — we carry the most common failure parts (capacitors, motor seals, salt cell flow switches, heater igniters) on every truck. Diagnosis is free if you're already a weekly customer; one-time repair calls get a fixed diagnostic fee that's credited toward the work.

The honest math: a repair quote from a national chain almost always includes a 30-50% markup on parts plus a separate labor charge plus a trip charge. Our pricing is parts-at-cost plus flat labor — usually $300-$500 cheaper on the same job. We don't make money on parts; we make money on the relationship.

SERVICE 03 · GREEN-TO-CLEAN
Service / 03

Green-to-clean rescue.

Came home from vacation to a swamp. Skipped a service after a hurricane. Algae bloom after a heavy rainfall. Whatever the cause — we have a four-day clearing protocol that handles roughly 90% of green pools without draining. That matters because draining a Tampa pool risks structural damage from the high water table, and a refill plus restart is its own multi-day expense.

Day 1: shock + brush + initial filtration. Day 2: chemistry adjust + clarifier. Day 3: vacuum settled debris + filter clean. Day 4: balance + final clarity check. Then we hand you a clean pool and an honest conversation: if your usage warrants it, we move you onto weekly. If not, we don't push it. About 60% of green-to-clean customers stay with us afterwards because they've already seen what good service looks like.

  • Four-day protocol
  • No draining required
  • Storm recovery
  • Vacation neglect cleanup
  • Algae elimination
  • Filter rebuild if needed
Clearing Time 4 days typical
Cost $485-$750
Conversion No pressure
SERVICE 04 · EQUIPMENT INSTALL
Service / 04

Equipment install.

Variable-speed pump upgrades are the highest-ROI improvement you can make to a Tampa pool — they cut energy bills 60-80% versus old single-speed pumps and pay back in 18-24 months. Florida code now requires VS pumps for any new install or major replacement, and a lot of older Tampa pools are still running pre-2015 single-speed motors.

Beyond pumps: salt-system conversions (cuts chlorine cost ~80%, stops the bleach-stink), LED color lighting, automation upgrades (Pentair IntelliCenter, Hayward OmniLogic), heater swaps from gas to heat-pump for snowbirds. We handle permitting, install, and commissioning. Most jobs done in a single day. We don't markup parts — install pricing is parts-at-cost plus flat labor.

  • Salt system conversions
  • LED color lighting
  • Automation install
  • Heater install / swap
  • Permit handling

SERVICE 05 · INSPECTION
Service / 05

Inspection & acid wash.

Buying a Tampa house with a pool? A real pool inspection is not a five-minute walk-around. We do a 90-minute pre-purchase inspection that covers equipment age and condition, plumbing pressure-test for leaks, surface condition (looking for hairline cracks, hollow tile, plaster failure), chemistry history if the seller has records, and a written report you can take to negotiation. Our inspections have caught $4K-$15K of needed repairs that the seller's listing never disclosed.

Acid wash is a separate service for surface staining (calcium scaling, organic stains from leaves, mineral discoloration). We drain partial, treat the affected areas with muriatic acid, neutralize, and refill. Most jobs done in 6-8 hours. Done right, surface staining doesn't return for years; done wrong, it eats into the plaster and shortens the pool's life.

  • Pre-purchase inspection
  • Equipment evaluation
  • Leak detection
  • Surface assessment
  • Acid wash for staining
  • Written report & photos

Snowbird watch.

Our snowbird watch program is built specifically for seasonal residents: we run a tightened-up service while you're gone, prep for storms, and have your pool swim-ready the day you fly back in.

Premium tier adds full weekly maintenance equivalent and house-watch perimeter check (mail pickup, trash bin in/out, package retrieval). About 20% of our customer base is snowbird — we built the program around what they actually need.
