SERVICE 01 · INTERIOR REPAINT
Service / 01

Interior repaint.

Standard interior repaint is three days on-site for a typical 1,800-2,400 sq ft home. Day one is prep — masking, drop cloths, patching nail holes and drywall damage, sanding, priming bare patches and drywall repairs. Day two is paint — two coats minimum, cut-in then roll, ceilings first if included. Day three is touch-up, walkthrough, color-card delivery.

What we use: Sherwin-Williams Cashmere or Emerald (interior premium lines), Benjamin Moore Regal Select or Aura. We do not push the contractor-grade discount paint that "covers in one coat" — it doesn't, and it fails in two years. The 18% premium for premium paint is what hits the five-year color warranty.

  • Premium paint lines
  • Free color consult
  • 8x10 sample boards
  • Trim & ceiling included
  • 5-year color warranty

SERVICE 02 · EXTERIOR REPAINT
Service / 02

Exterior repaint.

Full house exterior is 4-7 days depending on size and condition. Always starts with a pressure wash — Florida pollen, mildew, salt residue near coastal homes — none of it bonds to the existing paint, but new paint won't bond to it either. Then prime bare wood, scrape any peeling existing paint, caulk gaps, repaint with a UV-stable exterior premium line.

Florida UV destroys cheap exterior paint in 18 months. We use Sherwin-Williams Resilience or Emerald exterior, Benjamin Moore Aura exterior — paints engineered for high-UV climates with 25-year manufacturer warranties. Our 5-year color warranty is the labor side; the paint manufacturer's warranty covers the product.

  • Pressure wash always
  • UV-stable premium
  • Two coats minimum
  • Soffits, fascia, trim
  • Doors & shutters
  • Manufacturer + labor warranty
Per sq ft $3.85-$7.20
Whole House 4-7 days
Warranty 5 + 25 yr
SERVICE 03 · CABINET REFINISH
Service / 03

Cabinet refinish.

The right way to refinish kitchen cabinets: doors and drawer fronts removed, hauled to our shop, sprayed in a controlled dust-free booth, dried under climate control, returned and re-hung. Boxes scuff-sanded in place, primed, sprayed on-site (we tent the kitchen and exhaust the area). Hardware re-attached with new screws. Three-week turnaround.

What you don't want: brush-painted cabinets done in your kitchen. Brush marks always show in raking light, the finish never cures hard enough to resist nicks, and the kitchen is unusable for a week. The shop-spray process is what gets the factory-finish look that lasts.

  • Doors sprayed in booth
  • Boxes sprayed on-site
  • Climate-controlled cure
  • New hardware screws
  • Color matching available
  • 3-year cabinet warranty
Per door $95-$175
Avg Kitchen 3 weeks

SERVICE 04 · DECKS & STAIN
Service / 04

Decks & fence stain.

Wood decks in Florida are unforgiving — UV bleaches the surface, afternoon thunderstorms swell and shrink the boards, and a south-facing deck can lose its finish in 18 months. We strip the existing finish if there is one (chemical strip or sand depending on condition), sand smooth, then apply a penetrating stain that bonds to the wood rather than sitting on top.

Penetrating stain options: solid (full color, hides grain — best for older damaged decks), semi-transparent (shows grain through tinted finish — best for newer decks), or natural (clear protective finish — best for ipe and other premium hardwoods). Pressure-treated, cedar, ipe, and composite (yes, composite can be re-stained).

  • Strip existing finish
  • Sand smooth
  • Penetrating stain
  • Solid, semi, or natural
  • All wood species
  • 3-year stain warranty
Per sq ft $3.40-$5.20
Standard Deck 2-3 days

SERVICE 05 · PRESSURE WASH & PREP
Service / 05

Pressure wash & prep.

Standalone pressure wash service when you want to clean the house, driveway, pool deck, or patio without committing to a full repaint. Two methods: soft-wash for delicate surfaces (cedar siding, painted aluminum, screen enclosures, roofs — uses low-pressure detergent rather than high-pressure water), high-pressure for concrete and masonry (driveways, pool decks, paver patios, brick walls).

Eco-friendly biodegradable detergents that won't harm landscaping or pool chemistry. Drainage-aware on storm drains — we don't blast contaminated runoff into the city system. Most homes can be done in a single day. Often paired with exterior repaint as the prep step, but offered standalone for customers who just want the cleaning.

  • Soft-wash for delicate
  • High-pressure for hardscape
  • Eco detergents
  • Drainage-aware
  • Storm drain protection
  • 1-day standard turnaround

SERVICE 06 · HURRICANE REPAINT
Service / 06

Hurricane repaint.

Post-storm exterior repaint after a named hurricane. Wind-driven rain forces water behind paint and lifts whole sheets off Florida stucco and siding. The damage usually shows up 2-4 weeks after the storm — that's when it stops looking like surface debris and starts looking like a $12K paint job.

We work directly with insurance adjusters on storm claims (State Farm, Citizens, USAA, Tower Hill, most Florida carriers). Photos and damage assessment delivered to your adjuster within 48 hours of dispatch. Existing maintenance customers get priority scheduling — typically on-site within 5 days of named-storm activation. New customers calling cold during a storm typically wait 3-4 weeks.

  • Insurance direct billing
  • Adjuster documentation
  • Priority for existing
  • Full restoration paint
  • Not patch jobs
  • 5-year warranty
