What We Do

Every electrical job. One standard.

Seven specialties. Every job is run by a licensed master electrician, permit pulled, panel labeled, inspector-ready. Here's the full scope.

SERVICE 01

Panel Upgrades & Service Changes

If your home has a Federal Pacific or Zinsco panel, you've got a known fire hazard — and you're going to have a hard time selling. We do clean 100-to-200amp and 200-to-400amp upgrades in a single day, permit pulled and inspector-coordinated. Every breaker labeled by hand.

  • 100A to 200A upgrades
  • 200A to 400A upgrades
  • FPE / Zinsco panel replacement
  • Square D, Siemens, Eaton
  • Meter base replacement
  • Grounding & bonding
  • Power company coordination
  • Labeled panel & documentation
SERVICE 02

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 home charging, clean install. Load calc, panel check, conduit run, final commissioning. We work with all the major chargers — Tesla Wall Connector, ChargePoint, Emporia, JuiceBox, Wallbox, and more. Federal 30% tax credit eligible.

  • Tesla Wall Connector install
  • J1772 / NACS universal chargers
  • Load calculation included
  • Dedicated 240V circuit
  • Panel capacity review
  • Clean conduit routing
  • Wi-Fi app configuration
  • Tax credit documentation
SERVICE 03

Whole-Home Rewiring

If your house has knob-and-tube, aluminum branch circuits, or a two-wire ungrounded system, you need a rewire. We do it right — minimal drywall damage, code-compliant, inspector-approved, typically in 5-10 days for most homes.

  • Knob & tube replacement
  • Aluminum wiring remediation
  • Two-wire to grounded conversion
  • AFCI / GFCI throughout
  • New outlets & switches
  • Minimal drywall impact
  • Patch & paint included
  • Whole-house label & documentation
SERVICE 04

Generator & Transfer Switch

Whole-home standby generators. In Tampa Bay, when hurricane season hits, having a Generac or Kohler with an automatic transfer switch means your food stays cold and your AC stays on. We handle pad, gas line coordination, permits, and commissioning.

  • Generac PowerPro Elite dealer
  • Kohler & Briggs installs
  • Automatic transfer switch
  • Concrete pad prep
  • Gas line coordination
  • Whole-home or partial-load
  • Annual maintenance contracts
  • Warranty registration handled
SERVICE 05

Lighting & Smart Home

Recessed can lighting, landscape lighting, pendant installs, ceiling fans, and full smart-home wiring. Bring us a designer's lighting plan, we'll wire it exactly to spec. Lutron Caséta certified for smart-switch installations.

  • Recessed LED can lighting
  • Landscape & path lighting
  • Pendant & chandelier install
  • Ceiling fan installation
  • Lutron Caséta systems
  • Smart switch & dimmer
  • Dedicated lighting circuits
  • Outdoor & security lighting
SERVICE 06

Commercial & Tenant Build-Out

We work with general contractors, property managers, and business owners across Tampa Bay for tenant build-outs, three-phase service, and ongoing maintenance. Our project manager lives on her phone — you'll actually get call-backs.

  • Tenant build-out electrical
  • Three-phase service
  • Commercial panel upgrades
  • Dedicated equipment circuits
  • Emergency & egress lighting
  • Data / low-voltage prewire
  • Scheduled maintenance contracts
  • Permit pulls & inspection coordination
SERVICE 07

24/7 Emergency Service

Burning smell from the panel. Sparking outlet. Breaker that won't reset. Don't wait. Call (813) 555-0163 any hour — a licensed electrician picks up, and we dispatch within 60 minutes for active safety issues across Hillsborough and Pinellas.

  • Licensed electrician on call 24/7
  • 60-min emergency dispatch
  • Power restoration
  • Burning / smoking outlets
  • Sparking & arcing
  • Panel fire damage
  • Storm damage repair
  • Temporary service setup
