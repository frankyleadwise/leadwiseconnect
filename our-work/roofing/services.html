What We Do

Full-service roofing. No shortcuts.

Six specialties. One team. Every job walked by Daniel before quote, during install, and at final inspection. Here's the full breakdown of what we build — and what you get when you work with us.

Service 001

Residential Re-Roofing

Full tear-off and new installation for single-family homes across Tampa Bay. We remove your existing roof, inspect the decking for rot or storm damage, make necessary repairs, and install your new roof to current Florida hurricane code — often in a single day.

  • GAF Timberline HDZ shingles
  • 50-year manufacturer warranty
  • Synthetic underlayment standard
  • Ice & water shield at valleys
  • Full decking inspection
  • Code-compliant ventilation
  • Same-day completion typical
  • Lifetime workmanship warranty
Service 002

Storm & Hail Damage

After every major Tampa Bay storm, we offer free damage inspections. Our team documents everything your adjuster needs to see, meets them on-site, and advocates for a full approval — not just a patch. We've recovered over $4.2M in insurance claims for homeowners since 2019.

  • Free post-storm inspection
  • Full photo documentation
  • Direct work with your adjuster
  • Xactimate estimate provided
  • Deductible gap help (when legal)
  • Emergency tarp available 24/7
  • Insurance claim specialist on staff
  • No upfront cost to inspect
Service 003

Metal Roofing

Florida's extreme sun and hurricane conditions make metal the smartest long-term choice. Standing seam systems last 50+ years, reflect heat to reduce cooling costs, and often qualify for significant homeowner insurance discounts. We install standing seam, ribbed panel, and stone-coated steel.

  • Standing seam systems
  • Ribbed panel (R-panel)
  • Stone-coated steel tile
  • Florida hurricane-rated
  • 50-year lifespan typical
  • Energy Star reflective colors
  • Insurance discount qualified
  • Financing available
Service 004

Commercial Flat Roofs

TPO, modified bitumen, and silicone restoration coatings for warehouses, retail, multifamily, and mixed-use. We schedule installs around your operating hours so your tenants and customers never know we're there. 20+ commercial projects per year across the Bay.

  • TPO single-ply membrane
  • Modified bitumen systems
  • Silicone restoration coatings
  • EPDM rubber replacement
  • After-hours installation
  • Multifamily & HOA specialists
  • 20-year warranties standard
  • Annual maintenance plans
Service 005

Leaks & Repairs

Active leak? Call us now. We run a 24/7 emergency tarp service across the entire Tampa Bay region. Once the immediate leak is contained, we diagnose the root cause — flashing, shingle failure, valley separation, or decking rot — and repair what's actually broken, not what's easy to sell.

  • 24/7 emergency tarping
  • Same-day diagnostics
  • Flashing replacement
  • Valley repair
  • Shingle replacement
  • Decking repair
  • Skylight resealing
  • Full written repair report
Service 006

Roof Inspections

Buying a home? Selling one? Need documentation for your insurance carrier? We provide photo-documented inspection reports delivered within 48 hours. Covers remaining useful life, known issues, ventilation adequacy, flashing condition, and full decking assessment from attic access.

  • Pre-purchase inspections
  • Pre-listing inspections
  • Insurance documentation
  • 4-point inspection reports
  • Wind mitigation reports
  • Remaining life estimate
  • 48-hour report turnaround
  • Flat $275 inspection fee
