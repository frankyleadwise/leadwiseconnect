From the Owner

Built by a roofer, not a salesman.

My grandfather started laying shingles in 1961. My dad joined him in the late seventies. I started carrying bundles up the ladder when I was fourteen. That's how I know what a roof should actually look like — I've seen forty years of them, up close.

I started Cornerstone in 2009 because I was tired of watching homeowners get sold roofs they didn't need by companies that wouldn't be around in five years. Every single one of my crews has been with me for at least seven years. I still walk every final inspection personally.

If that sounds like a throwback — good. It is. That's the point.

— Daniel Reeves
