Common Questions

Before you call.
Quick answers.

If you don't see your question here, just call us — Linda will answer it on the phone in less time than it takes to type into a contact form.

How much does spring replacement cost in Tampa?

Standard residential spring replacement runs $285-$425 in Tampa Bay, depending on door size (single vs double-car), spring type (torsion vs extension), and whether the cables need replacement at the same time. We always replace springs in pairs — if one breaks, the other is at end of life. Replacing one alone almost guarantees a second emergency call within 6-12 months. Full quote on the phone before we dispatch.

How fast can you actually get a truck out?

For active emergencies during business hours (7am-7pm Mon-Sat): 60-180 minutes typical response time. Our average across all emergency calls in 2025 was 2.4 hours from call to wheels in driveway. Off-hours and Sunday emergencies have a small surcharge ($85) and longer response (3-6 hours typical) since we're dispatching from home.

What does the lifetime warranty actually cover?

Springs we install. If a spring we replaced breaks again — even ten years later — we replace it free of charge, no service-call fee on the warranty replacement, no fine print. The warranty is on every invoice in writing. If you sell the house, the warranty transfers to the new owner. The only thing that voids it is physical damage from impact (a vehicle hits the door, etc.) which is what insurance is for.

Do you charge a service-call or diagnostic fee?

No. The price quoted on the phone is the price on the invoice. We diagnose during the call (most issues we can identify in three questions — broken spring, off-track, opener). If we get on-site and find something genuinely different than what was described, we re-quote before any work starts. Most jobs match the phone quote exactly.

Will my homeowners insurance cover this?

Spring failure from age — no, that's normal wear. Storm damage, vehicle impact, vandalism — usually yes, depending on your deductible. We work directly with most major insurance carriers in Tampa Bay (State Farm, Allstate, Citizens, USAA, Travelers, Universal) on claim-related repairs. We send photos and damage assessment to your adjuster within 48 hours of dispatch. Direct billing on most claims.

Do you do new construction garage doors?

Yes — new construction installs and builder/contractor partnerships are about 15% of our annual business. We work with several Tampa Bay custom home builders. For homeowners doing a renovation that includes garage door replacement, we coordinate with your GC on permit timing and inspection scheduling.
