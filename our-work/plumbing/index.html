From the Owner

No gimmicks. Just honest work.

I started Honest Work in 2011 after spending twelve years working for the big plumbing chains. I watched them push techs to upsell every single call — even when the customer just needed a $40 cartridge. I was done with it.

So I built a plumbing company that pays its tradesmen hourly instead of commission. Where the master plumber actually shows up to your house — not a guy with a two-day certificate. Where you get the same technician on repeat calls because we think that's how trust gets built.

It's not complicated. It's just the way my granddad — who taught me how to sweat copper when I was fifteen — would've done it. That's the whole idea.
