What We Do

The full trade. Six disciplines. Six master plumbers.

All work done in-house by Florida-licensed master plumbers. No subs. No franchise tricks. No pressure upsells.

Service 01

Drain Cleaning & Rooter

Kitchen clogs, main line backups, slow shower drains, root intrusion into sewer laterals. We camera every main line before we clear — figure out whether it's a clog, a root, or a collapsed line. Most drain clears done in one visit. Starting at $185 flat.

  • Kitchen & bath drain clears
  • Main sewer line clearing
  • Hydro jetting
  • Root intrusion repair
  • Camera inspection (included)
  • Sewer line locating
  • Trenchless pipe lining
  • Rooter maintenance plans
Service 02

Water Heater Service & Replacement

Tank and tankless. Rheem, Rinnai, Bradford White, Navien, AO Smith. Same-day install in most cases. We pull the permit, install to code with proper pan and shutoff, haul the old unit off. 6-year tank warranty, 2-year labor warranty.

  • Tank water heater replacement
  • Tankless conversion & install
  • Expansion tanks & pans
  • Recirculation pumps
  • Anode rod replacement
  • Leak repair & element swap
  • Gas line installation
  • Permits pulled & inspection
Service 03

Repipes & New Installs

Galvanized steel replacement, polybutylene replacement, and full new construction plumbing. PEX-A or Type-L copper. We pull permits, pass inspections first-try, patch drywall, and warranty the pipes for 25 years. One-week typical turnaround for a 3-bed.

  • Whole-house repipe (PEX-A or copper)
  • Galvanized & polybutylene replacement
  • Drywall patch & finish
  • Permits & inspections
  • New construction rough-in
  • Water service replacement
  • 25-year pipe warranty
  • Phased work schedule
Service 04

Leak Detection & Repair

Slab leaks, pinhole copper leaks, hidden wall leaks, ceiling leaks. Acoustic and thermal imaging to locate before we cut. Minimum-cut approach saves you drywall, tile, and time. We re-patch what we open and leave the space the way we found it.

  • Acoustic leak detection
  • Thermal imaging
  • Slab leak repair
  • Pinhole copper repair
  • Hidden wall & ceiling leaks
  • Drywall patch included
  • Tile matching where possible
  • Insurance claim documentation
Service 05

Fixtures & Bath Remodels

Toilets, faucets, shower valves, tubs, bidet installs, garbage disposals, ice-maker lines. On bathroom remodels we can work as your plumbing sub — coordinating with your GC — or we'll run the project end-to-end with our trusted tile and electrical partners.

  • Toilet install & replacement
  • Faucet & shower valve
  • Tub & shower install
  • Bidet & luxury fixtures
  • Garbage disposal
  • Ice-maker & pot filler lines
  • Bath remodel plumbing
  • Full remodel project management
Service 06

Commercial Plumbing

Restaurants, offices, retail, medical, dental. Grease traps, annual backflow testing and repair, commercial water heaters, tenant buildouts, ADA-compliant retrofits. Service contracts with 24/7 emergency coverage and dedicated account management.

  • Grease trap service & replacement
  • Backflow testing & repair
  • Commercial water heaters
  • Restaurant plumbing
  • Medical & dental buildouts
  • ADA-compliant retrofits
  • 24/7 emergency contracts
  • Dedicated account manager
