Capabilities

WHAT WE HANDLE.

Six disciplines, all performed in-house with owned equipment and a W-2 crew. Ray Castellanos on the lead machine for every job over $15K. No subcontracting.

Service 01

SITE PREP & GRADING

Raw lot to slab-ready pad. Clearing, grubbing, topsoil strip, mass grading, compaction, fine grading to engineer specification. GPS-guided grade control when the job calls for it, laser screed for flat pads, surveyor relationship for punch-list sign-off.

  • Residential pad prep
  • Commercial site work
  • Driveway & paver base prep
  • Mass grading
  • Fine grading (±0.1 ft)
  • GPS grade control
  • Compaction testing
  • Erosion control (SWPPP)
Service 02

UNDERGROUND UTILITIES

FL-licensed Underground Utility Contractor (Lic. #CUC1226114). We pull the water, sewer, and stormwater permits ourselves, trench to spec, tie in cleanly, and test. No waiting on a sub to get on your schedule.

  • Water main taps & laterals
  • Sanitary sewer laterals
  • Stormwater systems
  • Electrical & data trenching
  • Manhole & catch basin install
  • Lift station excavation
  • Permit processing
  • Pressure & leak testing
Service 03

DEMOLITION

Residential teardowns, outbuilding removal, concrete slab & foundation rip-out, asphalt removal, pool demolition. DEP-compliant debris hauling with recycling where we can. Clean-to-grade every time — no leftover rebar, no buried slab pieces.

  • Residential demolition
  • Garage & shed teardown
  • Pool removal & fill
  • Concrete slab rip-out
  • Asphalt & driveway removal
  • Foundation removal
  • Abatement coordination
  • Clean-to-grade finish
Service 04

LAND CLEARING

Selective tree removal, forestry mulching, brush clearing, stump grinding. Arbor-smart — we work from the site plan and preserve what's specified. Acreage to infill lots. Full debris removal or on-site mulching if you want to reuse the chips.

  • Selective tree removal
  • Forestry mulching
  • Brush & underbrush clearing
  • Stump grinding & removal
  • Root raking
  • Tree protection fencing
  • Acreage clearing (up to 40 acres)
  • Debris hauling or on-site mulch
Service 05

DRAINAGE & RETENTION

Florida's a flat state with a lot of water. If the site has drainage problems we solve them — pond excavation, dry retention basins, swales, french drains, culvert installation. Designed for the engineer's spec, built for the next hundred-year storm.

  • Retention pond excavation
  • Dry detention basins
  • Swale construction
  • French drains
  • Culvert install
  • Headwall construction
  • Grading for water management
  • Flood remediation
Service 06

HAULING & FILL MATERIAL

Our own triaxle dump trucks. We haul import fill, dirt export, sand, shell, lime rock, crushed concrete, and screened topsoil. Loaded at the quarry, weighed at the site, tonnage documented. Transparent pricing — no trucking middle-man markup.

  • Fill dirt — import & export
  • Sand (concrete & masonry)
  • Shell & lime rock base
  • Crushed concrete (recycled)
  • Screened topsoil
  • Rip-rap & decorative stone
  • Tonnage documentation
  • Same-day delivery
