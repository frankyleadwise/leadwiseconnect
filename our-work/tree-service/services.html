SERVICE 02 · PRUNING & TRIMMING
Service / 02

Pruning & trimming.

ISA-spec pruning. Crown thinning to reduce wind load on mature live oaks before hurricane season (best done in May/June). Structural pruning on young trees to set up the canopy correctly for the next thirty years. Deadwood removal. Clearance pruning over rooflines, driveways, walkways, and sightlines.

What we don't do: top trees, lion-tail (strip the inner branches and leave only end-tufts), or remove more than 25% of the canopy in a single visit. Any of those will compromise the tree's structure long-term. If a "tree guy" quotes you topping, that's the tell — they're not arborists.

  • ISA-standard cuts
  • Crown thinning
  • Structural pruning
  • Deadwood removal
  • Clearance pruning
  • Pre-storm canopy reduction

SERVICE 03 · TREE REMOVAL
Service / 03

Tree removal.

Sectional removal in tight spaces — over pools, between houses, behind privacy fences, near power lines. We rig each cut, lower it controlled to the ground or to a designated drop zone, then chip or haul. For trees too big or too compromised for sectional, we bring in a crane (we partner with a Brandon-based 50-ton crane operator).

Stump options at quote: leave it, grind it, or full extraction (more expensive but eliminates root damage to nearby hardscape). Debris haul-off included unless you want the wood for firewood — happy to leave it cut and stacked.

SERVICE 04 · STUMP GRINDING
Service / 04

Stump grinding.

Below-grade grinding so you can replant or re-sod the area. Standard depth is 8-12 inches below the soil line — enough that grass roots won't hit anything for years and you can plant another tree nearby without conflict. We grind all visible surface roots out to 4-6 feet from the stump in most cases.

Chip removal options: haul off everything (most common), leave the chips for use as mulch elsewhere on the property, or backfill the hole with the chips and topsoil mix. Most stumps take 30-45 minutes including teardown of the access gate (yes, we put it back).

  • 8-12" below grade
  • Surface root grinding
  • Chip haul-off
  • Topsoil backfill option
  • Gate access standard
  • 30-45 min typical job
Per Stump $145-$385
Job Time 30-45 Min
Volume Discount 3+ Stumps
SERVICE 05 · PALM SPECIALIST
Service / 05

Palm specialist.

Palms aren't trees. Different growth pattern, different cuts, different problems. Sabal palms (the Florida state tree) handle hurricane wind beautifully but throw old fronds you need to clear. Royal palms shed enormous fronds that can damage cars. Queen palms get fungal infections that look like brown spots on the trunk and need diagnosis. Date palms produce heavy seed pods that crack driveways when they fall.

We're trained on every palm species commonly planted in Tampa Bay. Annual frond and seed-pod cleanup is the bread-and-butter. Disease assessment when something looks off. Pre-hurricane canopy reduction on large royals before storm season.

  • Sabal, Royal, Queen, Date
  • Foxtail & Pygmy specialists
  • Annual frond cleanup
  • Seed pod removal
  • Fungal disease assessment
  • Pre-storm canopy work
Per Palm $95-$285
Annual Plan 5+ Palms
Diagnosis Free On-Site
SERVICE 06 · HEALTH INSPECTION
Service / 06

Tree health inspection.

Pre-purchase property inspection — buyer's agent flagged the trees. Insurance company requesting tree health documentation after a storm claim. Probate/estate sale needing tree documentation. HOA or municipality requiring an arborist sign-off before tree removal permits. We walk the property, document each significant tree by species and condition, identify rot, root issues, structural problems, lean direction, and produce a written report with priority recommendations.

The signature on our reports is ISA-certified. That's the credential that insurance adjusters, real estate professionals, and Hillsborough/Pinellas tree commissioners actually accept. Anybody can write up a tree report — only ISA arborists can sign one that gets used as documentation.

  • Insurance documentation
  • Permit support
  • HOA arborist sign-off
  • Hazard tree assessment
  • Written ISA-signed report
