The Backstory

Built on a refund that never came.

In 2010, Marco and Elena bought their first house in Seminole Heights. Within six months, drywood termite swarmers came out of the upstairs bedroom doorframe. They called the national chain everyone calls. The tech sprayed baseboards, charged $1,400, and left.

Six weeks later: more swarmers. Second visit, $1,400 more. Sprayed again. Six weeks later, swarmers again. Third visit. Same routine. The company refused to refund anything because their terms and conditions said spot treatment couldn't carry a warranty.

Marco was a licensed applicator at a commercial pest company at the time. He knew what the right protocol was — fumigation tent for drywood termites, not baseboard spray. He paid out of pocket for the proper fumigation, then started taking on residential jobs nights and weekends.

By 2013 it was a full company. Tideline's whole pitch is the company that should have shown up at his house in 2010. Free inspection, written diagnosis, the right protocol for the actual species, real warranty, real callbacks. No upsells to a quarterly contract you don't need.

Operating Principles

Four rules.
Non-negotiable.

These are the principles every Tideline technician operates under. They are the difference between a company that earns repeat business and a company that earns a complaint.

/ 01

Diagnose before treating.

Every job starts with an inspection. We never spray first and ask questions later. Wrong species, wrong product, wrong placement — all of those waste your money and our reputation.

/ 02

The treatment fits the pest.

Different pests, different protocols, different products. We don't run the same chemical on every job because it's easier for our supply chain. We carry seven different formulations on every truck.

/ 03

Same technician, every visit.

If Carlos treated your house in spring, Carlos comes back in summer. He knows your kitchen layout, where the activity was, what worked, what didn't. No starting from zero with a stranger.

/ 04

The warranty is in writing.

Every invoice has a warranty clause. Termite work: 7 years renewable. Treatment work: 30-day callback. Quarterly: free re-treatment between visits. No fine print, no carve-outs.
