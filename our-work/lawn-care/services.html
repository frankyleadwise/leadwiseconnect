What We Offer

Design, install, maintain. End to end.

Six disciplines. All performed in-house by a certified landscape professional and a crew we've trained ourselves. No subs, no franchise gimmicks, no catalog plants.

Service i

Lawn & Garden Maintenance

Weekly or biweekly service for established properties. Our maintenance model isn't commodity mowing — it's full stewardship: selective pruning on the right plants at the right time, seasonal fertilization cycles, pest monitoring, and debris cleanup. Same crew every visit.

  • Mowing, edging, blowing
  • Selective pruning & shaping
  • Seasonal fertilization
  • Weed control (spot treat)
  • Mulch refresh (2x/year)
  • Seasonal color rotation
  • Pest & disease monitoring
  • Annual turf aeration
Service ii

Landscape Design & Install

Full-property redesigns drawn by a state-certified landscape professional. We start with a walkthrough, a soil & sun survey, and a conversation about how you actually live outside. Then a plan, a plant list, and a phased installation. Florida natives specified wherever they'll perform.

  • Concept + scaled plan drawings
  • Native Florida plant palette
  • Shade tree placement & selection
  • Hedgerow & privacy screening
  • Perennial bed design
  • Grading & drainage review
  • Phased installation available
  • 1-year plant health warranty
Service iii

Hardscape & Stonework

Paver patios, flagstone walkways, retaining walls, fire pits, dry creek beds. Built by hand on a proper base — compacted fill, geotextile fabric, polymeric sand joints. The walkway your grandkids will still walk on.

  • Paver patios & walkways
  • Natural stone & flagstone
  • Retaining walls (block & boulder)
  • Fire pits & outdoor fireplaces
  • Dry creek beds
  • Step & stoop construction
  • Pergolas & shade structures
  • Edging & garden curbing
Service iv

Irrigation & Drainage

Florida-licensed irrigation work. We run system audits for established properties, install smart Rachio and Rain Bird controllers, and convert spray zones to drip where the plants prefer it. On the drainage side — french drains, dry wells, swales — we solve the water that ends up where it shouldn't.

  • Zone audits & efficiency reviews
  • Smart controller install
  • Drip irrigation retrofit
  • Head replacement & calibration
  • Backflow preventer install
  • French drains & dry wells
  • Grading for water management
  • FL Lic. IR-4412
Service v

Landscape Lighting

Low-voltage LED design — path lighting, accent up-lights, step lighting, architectural wash. We design around the home, not against it. Hidden transformers, dimmable zones, astro-timers. No floodlights glaring at the neighbors.

  • Path & walkway lighting
  • Tree & architectural up-lights
  • Step & stair lighting
  • Deck & patio lighting
  • Zone-based control & dimming
  • Astro-timer programming
  • Transformer & wire hiding
  • Smart home integration
Service vi

Commercial Grounds Management

HOAs, office parks, retail centers, boutique hotels, and restaurants. We scope the contract around your property's calendar, assign a dedicated account manager, and maintain photo logs so you can show your board exactly what's happening month-over-month.

  • Full-service grounds contracts
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Seasonal color rotation
  • Storm response & cleanup
  • Irrigation management
  • Tree trimming & removal
  • Budget planning & forecasting
  • Photo logs & reporting
