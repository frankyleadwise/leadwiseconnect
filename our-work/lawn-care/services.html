What We Do

Full-service grounds. Six disciplines, one crew.

All six services performed in-house by a FCLP-certified designer and a crew we've trained ourselves. No subs, no franchise tricks, no shortcut operators.

Service 01

Weekly Lawn Maintenance

Weekly or biweekly service for Tampa Bay homes and properties. Mow, edge, blow, selective pruning, seasonal fertilization, pest monitoring. Same crew every visit, on the same day. Starting at $60/week for standard Tampa lots.

  • Mowing, edging, blowing
  • Selective pruning & shaping
  • Seasonal fertilization
  • Weed control (spot treat)
  • Mulch refresh (2x/year)
  • Seasonal color rotation
  • Pest & disease monitoring
  • Annual turf aeration
Service 02

Landscape Design & Install

Full-property redesigns drawn by a FCLP-certified landscape professional. Walkthrough, soil/sun survey, written plan, phased installation. Florida natives specified wherever they perform. 1-year plant health warranty on every install.

  • Concept & scaled plan drawings
  • Native Florida plant palette
  • Shade tree placement
  • Hedgerow & privacy screening
  • Perennial bed design
  • Grading & drainage review
  • Phased installation available
  • 1-year plant health warranty
Service 03

Hardscape & Stonework

Paver patios, flagstone walkways, retaining walls, fire pits, dry creek beds. Built by hand on a proper base — compacted fill, geotextile fabric, polymeric sand joints. The walkway still looks tight at year ten.

  • Paver patios & walkways
  • Natural stone & flagstone
  • Retaining walls
  • Fire pits & outdoor fireplaces
  • Dry creek beds
  • Step & stoop construction
  • Pergolas & shade structures
  • Edging & garden curbing
Service 04

Irrigation & Drainage

Florida-licensed irrigation work (IR-4412). Zone audits, smart Rachio and Rain Bird controllers, drip conversion on planting beds, backflow preventers. On the drainage side — french drains, dry wells, swales — we solve standing water.

  • Zone audits & efficiency
  • Smart controller install
  • Drip irrigation retrofit
  • Head replacement & calibration
  • Backflow preventer install
  • French drains & dry wells
  • Grading for water management
  • FL Lic. IR-4412
Service 05

Tree Work & Hurricane Prep

Shade tree trimming, palm cleanup, canopy thinning, hurricane prep, post-storm cleanup. ISA-certified arborist on staff. $2M insured. We can't guarantee a tree won't fall in a major storm — but we can make sure it's not because of neglect.

  • Tree trimming & shaping
  • Palm tree cleanup
  • Canopy thinning
  • Dead & diseased removal
  • Hurricane prep pruning
  • Post-storm cleanup
  • Stump grinding
  • ISA-certified arborist
Service 06

Commercial Grounds

HOAs, office parks, retail centers, boutique hotels, restaurants. We scope contracts around your property's calendar, assign a dedicated account manager, and keep photo logs so you can show your board exactly what's happening month-over-month.

  • Full-service grounds contracts
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Seasonal color rotation
  • Storm response & cleanup
  • Irrigation management
  • Tree trimming & removal
  • Budget planning
  • Photo logs & reporting
