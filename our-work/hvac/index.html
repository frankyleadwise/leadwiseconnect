Why Ironclad

HVAC is trust. You deserve a tech that earns it.

Half the AC calls we get are fixing someone else's botched install — undersized ducts, mismatched systems, refrigerant undercharged from day one. It's why the system died in three years instead of fifteen. We're the opposite of that.

  • NATE-certified on every call Our techs hold North American Technician Excellence certification. The gold standard. Not a weekend course.
  • Manual J & D load calculations Before we quote a system, we calculate the house. Undersized units freeze up. Oversized ones short-cycle. We size it right.
  • Upfront flat-rate pricing You approve the price before we turn a wrench. No hourly surprises. No padded invoices. Parts listed with markup disclosed.
  • We label your thermostat Sounds small. It's not. Every system we install gets a printed label with the model, tonnage, filter size, and our phone.

From the Owner

Twenty years on Tampa roofs. I know what breaks and why.

I started as a helper in 2004, got my EPA 608 in 2007, and my NATE Master in 2011. Started Ironclad in 2011 out of my garage in Seminole Heights with one van and a window unit for a sign.

My wife Stacy runs the office, my brother-in-law Luis runs the install crew, and I run the service side. It's family, and it feels that way when you call — not like a call-center pitching you warranty extensions. That's the whole idea.

Tampa heat doesn't mess around. A system that works in Jacksonville gets cooked here. We spec heavier than code, we oversize the return, and we size the dehumidifier for the reality of a Gulf-coast home. That's why our installs last.

How It Works

Four steps. Straight talk.

01

Call or book online

Stacy answers. We triage — cooling failure, intermittent, smell, noise, whatever. Dispatch a tech the same day in most cases. Emergency service 24/7.

02

On-site diagnosis

Full check — pressures, amp draw, coil status, refrigerant. We show you the reading. No theatrics, no "it's worse than we thought" conversations.

03

Flat-rate quote

Written. Line-itemed. You approve before we turn a wrench. If it's a replacement call, Manual J happens before the bid — not after the sale.

04

Fixed & warrantied

Labor warranted 2 years on repair, 10 years parts on new installs. We label the thermostat and leave you the serial numbers. No "call this other number" handoffs.
