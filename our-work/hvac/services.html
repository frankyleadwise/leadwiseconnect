What We Do

Every HVAC job. One standard.

Six specialties. Every job run by a NATE-certified tech. Here's the full scope of what Ironclad handles across Tampa Bay.

Service 01

24/7 Emergency Service

When it's 94° outside and your AC stops blowing cold — we answer. Our emergency line is staffed around the clock by a human being, and we dispatch within 60 minutes anywhere in Hillsborough, Pinellas, and Pasco. Diagnostic fee waived when we do the repair.

  • 60-minute dispatch guarantee
  • Nights, weekends, holidays
  • System not cooling
  • Frozen coils / ice
  • Electrical failures
  • Capacitor blowouts
  • Leaking refrigerant
  • Waived diagnostic with repair
Service 02

System Install & Replacement

Full system replacements done right. Every install starts with a Manual J load calculation — not a guess. We install Trane, Carrier, Lennox, and Goodman. SEER2-compliant, permit-pulled, and matched with proper line sets so your system lasts 15+ years, not 5.

  • Trane Comfort Specialist
  • Manual J load calculation
  • SEER2-compliant systems
  • Heat pump & straight-cool
  • Mini-split installs
  • Variable-speed options
  • Smart thermostat included
  • 10-year parts warranty
Service 03

Diagnostic & Repair

Won't cool, short-cycling, noisy condenser, frozen coil, high humidity. We diagnose what's actually wrong — not what pushes your ticket to a replacement quote. Over 80% of our service calls end with a repair in the same visit.

  • Capacitor / contactor swap
  • Refrigerant leak repair
  • Coil replacement
  • Compressor diagnosis
  • Thermostat / control board
  • Drain line clearing
  • Fan motor replacement
  • Written warranty on all repairs
Service 04

Ductwork & Indoor Air Quality

Half the homes in Tampa Bay have ducts that were never sized right for the system. We redesign, rebuild, and seal ducts with proper static pressure — plus UV germicidal lights, HEPA filtration, and whole-home dehumidifiers for the mold-prone Florida summer.

  • Full duct redesign / rebuild
  • Aeroseal duct sealing
  • Static pressure testing
  • Return & supply sizing
  • UV germicidal lights
  • Media air cleaners
  • Whole-home dehumidifiers
  • Duct cleaning
Service 05

Preventive Maintenance

Twice-a-year tune-ups that actually catch things. Pre-summer and pre-winter visits include coil cleaning, refrigerant check, capacitor testing, drain flush, and a full written report. Our members get priority scheduling and discounted repairs.

  • 2 tune-ups per year
  • Condenser coil cleaning
  • Evaporator coil check
  • Refrigerant pressure verification
  • Capacitor load testing
  • Drain line flush
  • Priority emergency dispatch
  • 15% off all repairs
Service 06

Light Commercial

Restaurants, retail, multifamily, office. We service rooftop packaged units, mini-split arrays, and VRF systems. Scheduled maintenance contracts with 24/7 emergency priority for contract clients — because when the AC dies in a restaurant, you're losing money by the hour.

  • Rooftop unit service
  • Mini-split & VRF install
  • Restaurant refrigeration
  • Walk-in cooler service
  • Multifamily maintenance
  • Scheduled PM contracts
  • Priority emergency dispatch
  • Energy usage audits
