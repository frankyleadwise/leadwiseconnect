What We Do

Six disciplines. One NATE-certified crew.

All services performed in-house by NATE-certified technicians. No subs, no franchise tricks, no load-calc shortcuts.

Service 01

AC Repair & Diagnostics

Same-day service in most of Tampa Bay. 24/7 emergency dispatch. We arrive with a fully-stocked truck — capacitors, contactors, motors, common refrigerants — so most repairs are done in one visit, not four.

  • No-cool diagnostics
  • Capacitor & contactor swaps
  • Blower motor & fan replacement
  • Refrigerant leak detection
  • Frozen coil & airflow issues
  • Thermostat troubleshooting
  • Drain line unclogging
  • 24/7 emergency service
Service 02

System Replacement

Full AC and furnace swaps. Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, American Standard. Every quote comes with a proper Manual J load calculation — we don't just match your old unit and hope. Financing options available.

  • Manual J load calculation
  • Carrier, Trane, Lennox certified
  • Heat pumps & gas furnaces
  • Variable-speed systems
  • 10-year parts warranty
  • 2-year labor warranty
  • 0% financing available
  • Old unit removal included
Service 03

Ductwork & Airflow

Most "AC problems" in older Tampa homes are actually duct problems — leaky runs, undersized returns, bad design. We do Manual D duct design, AeroSeal sealing, zoning for multi-story homes, and return air rebalancing.

  • Manual D duct design
  • AeroSeal duct sealing
  • Zoning & dampers
  • Return air rebalancing
  • Flex-duct replacement
  • Plenum rebuilds
  • Insulation upgrades
  • Airflow measurement & testing
Service 04

Preventive Maintenance

Two annual tune-ups — spring (cooling) and fall (heating). Coil wash, refrigerant pressure check, electrical test, drain line flush, filter change, safety inspection. $189/year. Keeps your warranty valid and your system alive twice as long.

  • Semi-annual tune-ups
  • Coil cleaning (indoor + outdoor)
  • Refrigerant pressure check
  • Electrical connection testing
  • Drain line flush
  • Filter replacement
  • Safety inspection
  • Priority emergency dispatch
Service 05

Indoor Air Quality

Tampa humidity is brutal. We install whole-home dehumidifiers (the #1 indoor air quality fix nobody talks about), MERV-13 filtration retrofits, and UV-C coil purifiers. We measure humidity on every service call and tell you the truth about it.

  • Whole-home dehumidifiers
  • MERV-13 filtration
  • UV-C coil purifiers
  • HEPA air scrubbers
  • ERV / HRV ventilation
  • Duct cleaning & sanitizing
  • Radon mitigation integration
  • Indoor air quality testing
Service 06

Commercial HVAC

Office buildings, medical offices, retail, restaurants, warehouses. Rooftop units, split systems, VRF, mini-splits for buildout work. Quarterly PM contracts, dedicated account manager, 24/7 emergency coverage, direct dispatch — no call-center middleman.

  • Rooftop unit service & replacement
  • VRF / mini-split systems
  • Tenant buildout HVAC
  • Quarterly PM contracts
  • Dedicated account manager
  • 24/7 emergency dispatch
  • Refrigerant compliance
  • Building automation integration
