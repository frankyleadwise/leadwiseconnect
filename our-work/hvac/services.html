What We Do

Full-stack HVAC. Six disciplines, one crew.

Six services, performed in-house by NATE-certified technicians. No staffing agencies. No subs. No "uncertified apprentices" on your roof.

Service 01

AC Repair & Diagnostics

$89 diagnostic, waived on any approved repair. Full-system inspection, not just the obvious part. Same-day service when we have a truck in your area. Most common Tampa summer failures: capacitor, contactor, low refrigerant, drain pan, thermostat, TXV.

  • Capacitor & contactor replacement
  • Refrigerant leak detection
  • TXV & metering device work
  • Condensate drain & pan fixes
  • Thermostat replacement & wiring
  • Blower motor & fan replacement
  • Same-day when available
  • 24/7 emergency service
Service 02

System Replacement

Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Rheem. Manual J load calc run on every install — we don't just match the tonnage of what you already had. Most Tampa homes we replace turn out to be oversized. 10-year parts & labor warranty. Financing available.

  • Manual J load calculation
  • Manual D ductwork sizing
  • Trane / Carrier / Lennox / Rheem
  • Variable-speed / two-stage options
  • Heat pump & gas furnace options
  • 10-year parts & labor warranty
  • Financing via Synchrony
  • 1-day standard install timeline
Service 03

Ductwork & Airflow

Static pressure testing, duct sealing, redesigns for older homes. If one room in your house is always ten degrees hotter, the problem is almost always ductwork — not the AC. We measure airflow at each register, run a duct-blaster leakage test, and fix the actual problem.

  • Static pressure diagnostics
  • Duct-blaster leakage testing
  • Aeroseal duct sealing
  • Duct replacement & redesign
  • Manual D sizing on retrofits
  • Zoning & dampers
  • Return air upgrades
  • Insulated flex & rigid options
Service 04

Preventive Maintenance

Twice-yearly tune-ups — spring and fall. Coil wash, capacitor check, refrigerant pressure reading, drain flush, electrical audit. The #1 cause of mid-summer breakdowns in Tampa is weak capacitors and dirty coils. Both are catchable in a 45-minute PM visit.

  • Coil wash (outdoor & indoor)
  • Capacitor capacitance test
  • Refrigerant pressure reading
  • Drain line flush & treatment
  • Electrical connections audit
  • Airflow & temp-split check
  • Filter change
  • Priority scheduling for plan members
Service 05

Indoor Air Quality

Tampa humidity is brutal. Even a new AC in a well-sealed home can still leave the house at 60% RH if the system isn't configured right. We measure RH, VOCs, and CO2 before we recommend. UV lights, whole-home dehumidifiers, MERV-13 media filters, ERVs.

  • RH & humidity audit
  • MERV-13 media filter install
  • UV light installation
  • Whole-home dehumidifiers
  • ERV & fresh air systems
  • Duct cleaning (certified)
  • CO2 / VOC monitoring
  • Allergy-focused configurations
Service 06

Commercial HVAC

Light commercial — restaurants, dental & medical offices, small retail, boutique hotels, professional offices. RTU replacement, chilled water, VRF/VRV systems. Service contracts with scheduled visits, photo documentation, and dedicated account manager.

  • RTU replacement (Trane, Carrier)
  • VRF / VRV system installs
  • Chilled water service
  • Quarterly PM contracts
  • Photo documentation
  • Dedicated account manager
  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Annual refrigerant audit (608)
